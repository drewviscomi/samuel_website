About

Built by hand.
Shaped by craft.

I'm Samuel Viscomi, a furniture maker, carpenter, and sculptor working out of Southeast Tennessee. Every piece I build starts with the material itself: the grain, the weight, the character of the wood.

I specialize in custom furniture, sculptural woodwork, and fine joinery. Whether it's a dining table your family will gather around for decades or a one-of-a-kind sculptural piece, I approach every project with the same care and attention to detail.
